About

The Davis Quantum Journal Club (DQJC) rises from the acknowledgment that Information Theory and Information Technologies have dramatically improved our ability to understand physical systems and exert control on them. The related research activity of the past 50 years has proven to be of wide relevance across the scientific community because of its intrinsic interdisciplinarity.
